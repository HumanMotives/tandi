Explore projects What is Human Motives?

Human music from nonhuman systems

Human Motives is an ongoing practice in modular synthesis, physical modeling, and generative composition. Each work is built as a system first, then documented through recordings, performance, and interactive experiments.

The focus is on clarity, repetition, and slow change. Some projects become full releases, others live as sketches, essays, or browser experiments that invite you to listen differently.
