DATA GRAVE

About Data Grave

Where musicians bury their unfinished tracks.

Data Grave is a small web project by Chris van der Linden. It’s a playful resting place for your unfinished audio ideas—drop a file, pick a fate (bury or cremate), add an epitaph, and let it go. Your entry appears on the public graveyard with minimal metadata.

How it works

  • Upload a .wav, .mp3, or .ogg.
  • Choose Burial (keeps a playable copy) or Cremation (no audio stored).
  • Add a short epitaph and commit the file.

Contact & feedback — Join the Discord to share thoughts or report issues.
