About The Data Grave

Welcome to The Data Grave—your tongue-in-cheek burial ground for unfinished music loops. Here we poke fun at those half-baked tracks you never quite finished, honor their fleeting existence, and gently nudge you to move on to something new.

Our little corner of the web is built for musicians who have too many “almost-hits” piling up on their hard drives. Drag in your wav, mp3 or ogg file, choose to bury or cremate, and let us give your loop a proper—if sarcastic—farewell.

Behind the scenes, we store a tongue-in-cheek record of every loop you lay to rest, complete with a witty epitaph and a random “detected genre” just for kicks. We hope it brings a smile (or a groan) to your face as you clear out your musical cobwebs.
